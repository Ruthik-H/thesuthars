Our Legacy

Our Story

Rooted in the rich artisan traditions of Rajasthan, The Suthars represent a legacy of master craftsmanship that spans generations. As traditional Karigars — skilled artisans whose hands have shaped wood and stone into works of lasting beauty — our family has carried forward an ancient art form, perfecting it with each generation.

Since 1985, we have proudly served Bangalore, transforming homes, offices, and commercial spaces into timeless sanctuaries of elegance. Every project we undertake is more than interior work — it is a testament to our heritage, a canvas where traditional Rajasthani woodworking techniques meet contemporary design sensibilities.

We believe that true craftsmanship cannot be rushed. Every joint, every finish, every detail is executed with the precision and patience that has been passed down through our family for decades. This is what sets us apart — not just the interiors we create, but the soul we pour into every space.

Who We Are

Company Profile

Simplicity with style — delivering exceptional quality through every stage of the project.

The Suthars, operating under Bhavan Interior, stands among Bangalore's most trusted interior contractors — renowned for creating timeless interior spaces that seamlessly blend aesthetic beauty with practical functionality. Our portfolio encompasses complete architectural interior solutions, from the initial consultation and conceptual planning through meticulous execution and flawless finishing.

We are privileged to work across a diverse spectrum of projects — from intimate residential apartments and expansive luxury villas to sophisticated corporate offices, dynamic retail spaces, and large-scale commercial environments. Each project, regardless of scale, receives the same unwavering attention to detail and commitment to perfection that defines our craft.
